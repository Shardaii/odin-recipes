Simple Smoothie

Description

This simple Strawberry Banana smoothie, combines the refreshing sweet taste of fruit to make a healthy yet delightful treat

Treat yourself on a hot summer day outside or even a relaxing evening inside with this thirst quenching smoothie!

Ingredients

  • Greek Yogurt
  • FROZEN Strawberries
  • Banana
  • Low fat milk
  • Honey
  • Vanilla

Steps

  1. Chop banana
  2. Place ingredients into blender
  3. Blend until smooth
  4. Pour into glass and ENJOY!

Avoid overblending!
