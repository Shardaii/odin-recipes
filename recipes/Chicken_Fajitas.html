Chicken Fajitas

Description

These simple to make chicken fajitas are a go to dinner for a solo or family dinner. The tender, seasoned, juicy chicken paired with fresh pepers and sweet onions will leave you wanting more.

Top 'em with cheese, lettuce, guac and you have yourself a real hardy meal.

Ingredients

  • 3 chicken breasts
  • 1 medium onion
  • 1 lime
  • 3 bell peppers: red, yellow, green
  • 3 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 tablespoon chili powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon smoked paprika
  • 1/2 teaspoon onion powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon onion powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon black pepper
  • 1/2 teaspoon cumin optional
  • salt to taste
